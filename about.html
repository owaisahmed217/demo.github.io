About FRESH N CLEAN

Fresh N Clean Fresh N Clean Laundry & Dry Cleaning LLC is the only approved and certified laundry service provider for Dubai Healthcare City. Established in 1985 we have a vast experience in Hospital Laundry Industry and having several hospitals, clinics and diagnostic centers and companies as satisfied clients across Dubai, we understand the minutest details required by a Hospital in their laundry service for example proper tagging, the clothes and linen washed with sterile and disinfected water, an aseptic method used for washing contaminated articles. We utilize best in class arranging and administration programming for planning clothing assignments. We are the one of a kind clothing administration that utilizations present day devices.
